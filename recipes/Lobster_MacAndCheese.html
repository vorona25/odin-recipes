Lobster Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

This lobster mac and cheese is ultra creamy macaroni with two types of cheese and plenty of chunks of lobster meat, all baked together to golden brown perfection

Mac and cheese is pretty great on its own, but when you add lobster to the mix, you end up with something spectacular

This lobster mac and cheese is ultra creamy macaroni with two types of cheese and plenty of chunks of lobster meat, all baked together to golden brown perfection.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 pound pasta
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped cooked lobster meat
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablesoons chives
  • cooking spray
