Lobster Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

This lobster mac and cheese is ultra creamy macaroni with two types of cheese and plenty of chunks of lobster meat, all baked together to golden brown perfection

Mac and cheese is pretty great on its own, but when you add lobster to the mix, you end up with something spectacular

This lobster mac and cheese is ultra creamy macaroni with two types of cheese and plenty of chunks of lobster meat, all baked together to golden brown perfection.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 pound pasta
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped cooked lobster meat
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablesoons chives
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Coat a 2 or 3 quart baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Melt 2 tablespoons of the butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add the flour, then whisk until combined, about 30 seconds.
  3. Pour in the water and whisk until smooth and just thickened.
  4. Add the milk and whisk until combined.
  5. Stir in the pasta, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika and pepper, then bring the mixture to a simmer.
  6. Cook for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally, until pasta is done.
  7. Turn the heat to low, then stir in the cheeses. Keep stirring until the sauce is smooth.
  8. Fold in the lobster meat. Transfer the pasta mixture to the prepared baking dish.
  9. Melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter, and stir it into the panko breadcrumbs.
  10. Sprinkle the breadcrumbs over the top of the pasta. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until topping is golden brown.
  11. Sprinkle with chives and garnish with addition chunks of lobster if desired. Serve immediately.
